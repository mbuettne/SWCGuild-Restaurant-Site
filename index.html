Restaurant Description

Buettner Brewing is the culmination of years of trial and error in home brewing and cooking. After years of family and friends telling him he should be a professional, Buettner Brewing was founded in 2020 by Master Brewer and Head Chef Troy Buettner. And while we have gone pro, stepping through our doors is still just like going to a neighbors house! We specialize in providing superior service in a comfortable atmosphere, and of course the best food paired with high quality drinks! Stop by today to see what friends and family have been raving about for years!
